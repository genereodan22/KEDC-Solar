BATTERY STORAGE

Storage batteries are a great way to maximize the efficiency of a solar energy system. They allow excess energy to be stored and used later, when the sun isn't shining. This helps to ensure that the system is running at its peak efficiency at all times. Storage batteries also provide a backup source of power in case of an outage. This ensures that the solar energy system can continue to operate even if the grid is down.
