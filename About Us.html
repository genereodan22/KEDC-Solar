Kennedy Energy and Development Corp.-Solar

Kennedy Energy and Development Corp. commenced its operations in June 2015 with the motivation to provide solutions and infrastructures that will augment daily Filipino living. The company is composed of a team of knowledgeable and skilled technopreneurs who aim to respond to the increasing demand for quality construction service providers and cutting edge technology for sourcing sustainable energy.
Kennedy Energy and Development Corporation design, distribute, and install Solar PV Systems in residential homes, commercial, industrial, and manufacturing businesses and establishments.
At Kennedy Energy and Development Corp., we are committed to providing our customers with the most innovative and cutting-edge technologies available. Our team of experts is dedicated to staying ahead of the curve and providing our customers with the most advanced solutions.
